WELCOME

This tool helps you analyze survey data exported from LimeSurvey. Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. Prepare Your Data: Export your survey data from LimeSurvey as CSV files. (Under "Responses," choose "Export" and save as a CSV file. The default settings should work fine.) For now it is only possible to compare 2 Survey groups with each other (2 Files!).
  2. Upload Surveys: Go to the Survey page and upload the exported files.
  3. View Analysis: After uploading, navigate to the Analysis and Graphs pages to explore results and visualizations.
  4. Customize Settings: Adjust color schemes and keywords for charts on the Settings page.
  5. Manage Sessions: Find and reload previous sessions on the Sessions page. Sessions are saved automatically while you work.

Explore the features and gain valuable insights from your survey data. If you encounter any issues, check the logfile.
